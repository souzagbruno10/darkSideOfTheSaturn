Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability, Authenticity and Legality are the cibersecurity fundamentals. Confidentiality is the premise that assures our datas never will be caught for ANYBODY. Neither cybercriminals, nor even our most dear ones like wifes, husbands and childrens. Integrity is the premise that assures our datas never will be corrupted in a traffic between server-client and vice versa. Availability is the premise that first of all depends on the side server to communicate so that all networks can acess service and get working flowing. Authenticity is the premise that assures that one person is the real person when acess some service like a bank account on the smartphone. In this case cryptography and two factor authenticity is a important tool to protect client's data. Finally,Legality is the premise that assures all activities on internet cannot cause harm to anyone.
